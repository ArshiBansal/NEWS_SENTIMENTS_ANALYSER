News Pulse is a dark-mode Streamlit app that:
- aggressively harvests RSS feeds before they can 403 you for sport
- throws headlines at VADER like it's still 2014 karma season
- confidently declares the market “bullish” even when your bags are in witness protection
- auto-categorizes everything so you can LARP as a macro whale
- looks disgustingly premium for ~300 lines of Python and pure audacity

- Sort by “Most negative” — perfect for peak despair-scrolling
- Glassmorphism + gradient dark theme (ugly dashboards are a crime against humanity)
